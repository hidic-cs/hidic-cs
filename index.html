Welcome to HiDiC-CS.

A mobile health assistant that helps individuals suffering from HIV/AIDS, Cancer and Diabetes to find the closest treatment center, order medicine, consult their doctors and get timely reminders to take medications.

How we are helping our users

HiDiC, aims to make it easy for those suffering from what we can say are among the major diseases in the world right now, by:

  1. Helping them order medicine from accredited drug stores around them, and have the medicine delivered to them on time.
  2. Managing their medication intake through reminders that they themselves can set in the app.
  3. Finding the nearest treatment centers based on their current location.
  4. Bringing the doctors nearer, i.e the doctors are only a chat away.
